Sport England Physical Activity Analysis
An exploratory data analysis of Sport England's Active Lives Adult Survey (2015–2024), examining trends in physical activity levels across England by gender and age group. Built with Python, pandas, and matplotlib.
Key findings:

Adult activity levels are at a record high (63.7%) in 2023–24
A persistent gender gap of ~5 percentage points exists across all years
Younger adults (16–24) are significantly more active than older groups, with activity declining steadily with age
Activity dipped during COVID (2020–21) before recovering strongly

Data source: Sport England Active Lives Adult Survey — publicly available at sportengland.org
